About Me

I graduated in drama and I am a computer programming student at George Brown College discovering the art behind the screen.

I was always fascinated about art and technology since I was younger, but my journey led me to study Drama. Throughout the program, the space that caught my interest the most was stage technologies: how to manipulate the light and sound systems. When I graduated, I became an arts teacher, but, deep inside, the desire to challenge myself and to learn computer technology was still alive.

I started my computer science learning process exploring online courses, especially The Odin Project, initially focusing on building fundamentals understanding about computer, and how everything is connected inside. I also could build a basic understanding about web development.

Driven by curiosity, the more I dug into this new journey, the more I was got involved with software development ecosystem. To learn more, and build stronger skills, I started the Computer Programming program at George Brown College. It has been important to boost the understanding of computer science, computer logic, database management system, CLIs, git and GitHub, and some programming languages, such as Javascript and C# to name a few.

Most importantly, this journey has given me the opportunity to connect the arts knowledge and technology together, using creativity while solving problems and building applications and web pages. Here is some of the technologies I have been working.
