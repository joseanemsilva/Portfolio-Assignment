About Me

I graduated in Drama and I am now completing a Computer Programming degree at George Brown College, discovering the art behind the screen. Although my academic background began in the arts, I was always fascinated by the intersection of creativity and technology, especially stage technologies like lighting and sound systems. After completing my degree, I worked as an arts teacher, but my curiosity for computing never disappeared. I began exploring online courses, such as The Odin Project, where I developed fundamental computer science concepts and an initial understanding of web development. The more I learned, the more I became drawn to the software development ecosystem.

Driven by this growing interest, I pursued the Computer Programming program at George Brown College to strengthen my technical foundation. Through the program, I have gained experience in computer logic, database management systems, command-line tools, Git/GitHub, and programming languages such as JavaScript and C#. Most importantly, this journey has allowed me to merge my artistic background with technology, using creativity to approach problem-solving, design meaningful user experiences, and build applications and web pages.
